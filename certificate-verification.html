About Certificate Verification

MITS provides a secure and reliable certificate verification system to help employers, educational institutions, and other organizations verify the authenticity of certificates issued by MITS.

How to Verify a Certificate

  1. Locate the 12-digit Certificate ID on the certificate
  2. Enter the Certificate ID in the verification form
  3. Select the certificate type
  4. Enter the candidate's name as it appears on the certificate
  5. Enter the issue date
  6. Click "Verify Certificate" to check authenticity

Certificate Security Features

  • Unique 12-digit Certificate ID
  • QR Code for quick verification
  • Digital signature
  • Holographic seal
  • Watermark
